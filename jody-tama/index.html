How to Play Jody-Tama

Objective

Win by either capturing the opponent's Master or moving your Master to the opponent's Temple square.

Setup

Each player starts with 4 Monks and 1 Master on their side of the 5x5 board. 5 move cards are dealt from a deck of 17 unique cards.

Gameplay

  • On your turn, choose one of your cards and move a piece according to that card's pattern
  • After moving, that card goes to the center and you receive the center card
  • Cards show movement patterns relative to the piece's current position
  • You can only move to empty squares or capture opponent pieces

Winning Conditions

  • Capture the opponent's Master
  • Move your Master to the opponent's Temple square (center of their back row)
